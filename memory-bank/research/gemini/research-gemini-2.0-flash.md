# Gemini 2.0 Flash Image Generation - Research Summary

## Can Gemini 2.0 Flash Generate Images?
**Yes.** Gemini 2.0 Flash includes native image generation capabilities, not just image analysis. It can:
- Generate images from text prompts
- Edit existing images through natural language instructions
- Maintain conversation context for iterative refinements
- Generate consistent story illustrations with stable characters and settings

## API Pricing
- **Cost per image:** $0.039 per image (1024x1024px)
- **Batch Mode:** 50% discount = $0.0195 per image (24-hour processing)
- **Free tier:** Google AI Studio provides 1,500 free images daily with lower rate limits (15 requests/minute)

## Resolution and Quality Options
- Standard output: Up to 1024x1024px
- Strong text rendering for advertisements, social posts, invitations
- Advanced reasoning creates realistic, detailed imagery
- Suitable for recipe/food illustration with world knowledge reasoning

## Gemini 2.0 vs. 2.5 Flash Comparison
**2.5 Flash improvements:**
- 2-3x faster image generation
- Multi-image fusion (combine up to 3 images)
- Enhanced character consistency across scenes
- Better conversational editing
- More photorealistic composition

**Pricing:** Identical at $0.039 per image

## Food Photography Quality
No specific reviews found, but sources indicate strong performance with realistic, detailed imagery through advanced reasoning. Direct API testing would be needed for definitive food photography evaluation.
