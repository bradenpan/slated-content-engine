# Slated — Product Overview

## What Is Slated?

**Slated is a smart dinner planning app that builds personalized, nutritious weekly meal plans tailored to how your household actually eats.** Users tell the app what they like, what they can't eat, what's in their kitchen, and how much time they have — and Slated generates a full week of recipes with a one-tap shopping list. The tagline: *Dinner on Autopilot.*

Available on iOS and Android.

---

## Deep Personalization

Slated doesn't generate generic meal plans. During an 8-step onboarding flow, the app learns everything it needs to plan meals the way the user would — if they had the time:

- **Dietary restrictions** (16 options): Vegetarian, vegan, pescatarian, keto, paleo, low carb, gluten-free, dairy-free, halal, kosher, and more. These are strictly enforced — Slated will never generate a recipe that violates a restriction.
- **Allergies** (12 options): Peanuts, tree nuts, dairy, eggs, wheat, shellfish, sesame, and more. Treated as safety-critical — allergenic ingredients are absolutely excluded from all recipes.
- **Nutritional focus** (12 options): High protein, high fiber, low cholesterol, anti-inflammatory, low glycemic, gut health, omega-3 rich, kid-friendly, hidden veggies, and more. Slated tailors ingredient selection to support these health goals.
- **Cuisine preferences** (20 options): Italian, Mexican, Thai, Indian, Japanese, Korean, Mediterranean, Ethiopian, Caribbean, and more. Meal plans only include cuisines the user has selected.
- **Cooking style / vibe** (6 options): Fast & easy, budget-friendly, kid-approved, gourmet, meal prep, or health-focused. This shapes the complexity, ingredient cost, and presentation of every recipe.
- **Kitchen appliances** (6 options): Air fryer, Instant Pot, slow cooker, rice cooker, blender, food processor. Slated incorporates recipes that use the appliances a user actually owns.
- **Family size**: 1–10 servings. All recipes are scaled automatically.

Every preference is editable at any time from Settings — no need to redo onboarding.

---

## Smart Meal Plan Generation

When generating a weekly plan, users have additional controls:

- **Max cooking time**: Adjustable slider from 20 to 120 minutes (default 45). Every recipe in the plan respects this limit.
- **Day selection**: Choose which days to plan for (skip nights you're eating out or ordering in).
- **Use your pantry**: Enter ingredients you need to use up (e.g., "4 lbs chicken thighs, half a bag of spinach"). Slated prioritizes building recipes around what's already in your kitchen, reducing waste and grocery costs.
- **Include favorites**: Slider to pull 0–7 previously saved favorite recipes into the new plan, so the household's go-to meals stay in rotation.

The result is a full week of nutritious, personalized dinners — not a generic list of recipes pulled from a database.

---

## Adjust Meals On the Fly

Every recipe can be modified after generation with natural-language instructions. Users type what they want changed, or tap one of six quick-action chips:

- "Make it vegan"
- "Add more vegetables"
- "Make it spicier"
- "Reduce cooking time"
- "Make it kid-friendly"
- "Create entirely new recipe"

Modifications respect all existing dietary restrictions and allergies. If a user is gluten-free and asks to "make it heartier," Slated won't add pasta.

Users can also regenerate individual days without redoing the entire week, swap meals between days, remove meals they don't want, or schedule favorites from their recipe library into any open slot.

---

## Recipe Library & Importing

Users can save any generated recipe to their favorites for reuse across future weeks. They can also import recipes from external sources:

- **Import from URL**: Paste a link to any recipe online and Slated extracts it into a structured recipe card.
- **Import from photo**: Snap a picture of a recipe (cookbook, magazine, handwritten card) and Slated converts it.

Saved recipes persist indefinitely and can be scheduled directly into future meal plans.

---

## Grocery Integration

Slated generates a consolidated, categorized shopping list from the week's meal plan — organized by department (produce, dairy & eggs, meat & seafood, bakery, pantry, frozen, spices, etc.). Users can:

- Filter the list by day of the week
- Check off items as they shop (checked items excluded from export)
- **Export directly to Instacart** — select a retailer, and unchecked items are sent to Instacart for delivery or pickup

Additional grocery delivery integrations are planned.

---

## Dinner Draft: Family Voting

For households where "what's for dinner" is a group decision, Slated offers Dinner Draft — a voting feature where the app generates a pool of extra recipe options (~2.5x the normal amount) and sends them to family members. Each person votes on recipes through a swipe-based interface (swipe right to approve, left to skip). The top-voted recipes are automatically assigned to the weekly plan.

Push notifications drive family members back into the app to cast their votes.

---

## Family Accounts

Multiple family members share a single meal plan. The primary account holder invites members via email and controls permissions (who can edit the plan, modify the shopping list, add favorites). All changes — checking off a shopping item, swapping a recipe — sync instantly across everyone's devices in real time.

## Monetization

- **Free tier**: 15 "Chef's Credits" (3 refilled weekly). Each plan generation, recipe modification, or shopping list creation costs 1 credit.
- **Sorted+ subscription**: Unlimited everything, no credit limits.
- **Credit packs**: One-time purchases for users who want more credits without subscribing.
- **Free trial**: 14 days, no credit card required. App Store pre-order users get an extended 30-day trial.
