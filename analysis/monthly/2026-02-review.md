**Analysis:**

Pillar 1 demonstrates clear market fit with 3.1% save rate and 0.8% CTR, both significantly above targets. The plan-level content resonates strongly, particularly pins focused on "weekly meal planning" and "family meal prep." This validates the core differentiation strategy.

Pillar 2 performs nearly as well (2.8% save rate), confirming the "family dynamics" blue ocean thesis. Pins addressing picky eaters and family mealtime stress consistently outperform.

Pillar 3 shows mediocre performance (1.8% save rate), confirming that commodity recipe content faces heavy competition on Pinterest. However, it still warrants 25% allocation as discovery-layer content.

Pillar 4 is a clear failure with only 1.2% save rate despite targeting high-intent meal kit users. The audience appears too narrow for Pinterest's discovery-based platform.

Pillar 5 performs adequately but with limited volume potential due to its customization focus.

**Key Strategic Question — Plan-Level vs. Recipe-Level:**

Plan-level content (Pillar 1) achieves 3.1% save rate and 0.8% CTR versus recipe-level content at 1.8% save rate and 0.3% CTR. This 72% higher save rate and 167% higher CTR strongly validates the differentiation strategy. Users clearly prefer comprehensive planning solutions over individual recipes.

## Strategy Update Recommendations

### Recommendation 1: Eliminate Pillar 4 and Redistribute Allocation
**Change:** Drop Pillar 4 entirely. Redistribute its 10% allocation: 5% to Pillar 1, 5% to Pillar 2
**Data:** Pillar 4 shows 1.2% save rate (48% below target) and 0.3% CTR (40% below target) with minimal impressions (3,261)
**Expected Impact:** Increase overall save rate by 0.2pp and CTR by 0.1pp
**Success Criteria:** Overall save rate >2.5% in Month 2

### Recommendation 2: Phase Out AI-Generated Images
**Change:** Stop using AI-generated images. Increase stock photo usage to 70%, template-only to 30%
**Data:** AI images: 1.8% save rate vs stock photos: 2.5% save rate, while costing $3/image
**Expected Impact:** Save $200-300/month, increase save rate by 0.1-0.2pp
**Success Criteria:** Maintain or improve save rate while reducing image costs to $0

### Recommendation 3: Double Down on Planning Keywords
**Change:** Increase "weekly meal planning" variants to 25% of all pins, add "meal planning for beginners"
**Data:** Planning keywords average 3.6% save rate vs 1.8% for recipe keywords
**Expected Impact:** Increase overall save rate to 2.6%+
**Success Criteria:** Planning-keyword pins maintain >3% save rate at higher volume
