This is Month 1. The account launched with 20 pins and generated 12 total impressions, zero saves, zero clicks, and zero outbound clicks. **These numbers are not alarming — they are expected for a brand-new Pinterest account with no domain authority, no follower base, and no content history.** However, the data reveals several structural problems that need immediate attention: the metadata pipeline is broken (all 20 pins are classified as "unknown" pillar and "unknown" content type), the account is severely under-posting relative to the strategy's 28 pins/week target (20 pins total in the entire month vs. the planned ~112), the board architecture is spread too thin across 8 boards with most holding 1-2 pins, and the image source strategy has drifted from the documented plan (strategy says all AI-generated; actual mix includes Unsplash, Pexels, and template sources). Before optimizing content strategy, the operational infrastructure must be fixed. The pipeline is not executing the strategy as designed.
